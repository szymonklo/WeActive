Be Active

Come on in to join us... All you need to do is sign up!

This initiative helps people be active.

You can join others to run, ride, trek or do any kind of physical activity together...

If You would like to practise, but don't like to do that on your own, and feel that gym exercises are also not for You, just try it...

It is free, what not only means that You do not pay anything, but it means that You are free to choose any activity anytime You want.

Don't have fixed workig hours? Have shift work? Like to change activities often?

Look for the activity that suits You the best.

And if You want, You can host your own activity.

Good luck!
